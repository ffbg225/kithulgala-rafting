Safety & Comfort

Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. All adventure activities are conducted by certified guides using top-quality equipment. Our riverside facility includes clean changing rooms, secure storage for valuables, and shaded relaxation areas. The buffet lunch is prepared in hygienic conditions with fresh, local ingredients. While we encourage participation in all activities, alternatives are available for those who prefer to take it easier. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or special requirements when booking.

What to Bring

  • Swimwear and change of clothes
  • Secure footwear that can get wet (sandals with straps or water shoes)
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses)
  • Towel and personal toiletries

  • Any personal medications
  • Cash for additional purchases (souvenirs, extra drinks)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we customize the activities?
Absolutely! While we have recommended activity combinations that work well together, you're welcome to choose any two activities from our offerings. Some activities may have age or fitness restrictions - our team can advise on the best options for your group when you book.
Is this suitable for young children?
Yes, with some considerations. The minimum age for most water activities is 6 years, and children must be accompanied by adults. We can adjust activities to be more child-friendly (gentler rafting sections, easier kayaking routes). The riverside relaxation areas are perfect for families with young children who need downtime between activities.
What if we have dietary restrictions?
We can accommodate most dietary needs with advance notice. Our buffet typically includes vegetarian options, and we can prepare separate meals for vegan, gluten-free, or other specific requirements. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when you make your booking.
What happens in case of bad weather?
We monitor weather closely and will advise if conditions may affect activities. Light rain usually doesn't impact the experience, but in case of severe weather, we may modify activities or reschedule your visit. Your safety is our priority, and we'll work with you to find the best solution.
