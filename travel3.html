WHITE WATER KAYAKING ADVENTURE Available Now

Experience the thrill of navigating the Kelani River's rapids in your own kayak with our expert-guided white water kayaking adventures in Kithulgala. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn or an experienced kayaker seeking new challenges, our professional instructors will tailor the experience to your skill level. Enjoy the ultimate combination of adrenaline and breathtaking scenery as you master the river's Class II-III rapids.

Experience Highlights

  • Professional instruction for all skill levels
  • High-quality kayaks and safety equipment provided
  • 5 km of exciting Class II-III rapids
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • Stunning views of Kithulgala's tropical landscape
  • Perfect for developing kayaking skills

Our kayaking adventures begin with a comprehensive safety briefing and paddling instruction. Beginners will learn essential kayaking techniques in calm water before progressing to gentle rapids, while experienced kayakers can challenge themselves on more technical sections of the river. All participants receive one-on-one coaching from our certified instructors throughout the journey.

We provide all necessary equipment including kayaks, paddles, helmets, and life jackets. Our kayaks are specially selected for stability and maneuverability in white water conditions. The adventure includes multiple stops at scenic points along the river, with opportunities to practice new skills in different water conditions under the guidance of our instructors.

Safety Information

Safety is our top priority. All participants receive comprehensive safety training before entering the water. Our instructors are certified in first aid and river rescue. We provide high-quality safety equipment including helmets, life jackets, and spray skirts. Minimum age is 14 years. Participants must be comfortable in water and able to swim. We assess water conditions daily and adjust routes accordingly.

What to Bring

  • Swimwear or quick-dry clothing
  • Secure footwear that can get wet
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Change of clothes and towel
  • Waterproof camera (optional)
  • Water bottle

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior kayaking experience?
No prior experience is necessary for our beginner courses. We start with basic paddling techniques in calm water before progressing to gentle rapids. For intermediate and advanced kayakers, we offer more challenging routes with technical instruction.
What's the difference between rafting and kayaking?
Kayaking offers a more personal experience where you control your own boat, while rafting is a team activity in a larger boat. Kayaks are more maneuverable and allow you to practice precise techniques. Many adventurers find kayaking more challenging but ultimately more rewarding as you develop personal skills.
What happens if I flip over?
Flipping is a normal part of kayaking, especially when learning. We teach wet exits (getting out of a flipped kayak) and self-rescue techniques during the initial training. Our instructors are always nearby to assist, and we practice in controlled environments before tackling rapids. You'll wear a life jacket at all times.
Can I bring my own kayaking gear?
You're welcome to bring your own paddle, helmet, or life jacket if you prefer. However, all personal equipment must be inspected by our staff for safety and suitability. We recommend using our kayaks as they're specifically selected for these river conditions.
