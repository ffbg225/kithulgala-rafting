MOUNTAIN BIKING ADVENTURE Available Now

Experience the thrill of mountain biking through Kithulgala's spectacular landscapes. This adventure takes you through lush jungle trails, scenic tea plantations, and breathtaking viewpoints. Our professional guides will lead you through routes suitable for your skill level while ensuring your safety at every turn. Whether you're a beginner or experienced rider, we have the perfect bike and trail for you.

Adventure Highlights

  • Ride through diverse terrain including jungle trails and tea plantations
  • Choose from beginner, intermediate, or advanced routes
  • High-quality mountain bikes with full suspension
  • Professional guides with extensive local knowledge
  • Breathtaking viewpoints perfect for photo stops
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention

Our mountain biking adventure begins with a safety briefing and bike fitting at our base camp. You'll have the opportunity to test your bike on a short practice track before heading out. Our guides will select routes based on your experience level and preferences, ranging from gentle forest paths to challenging downhill sections. Along the way, we'll stop at scenic viewpoints where you can rest and take in the stunning landscapes.

We provide all necessary equipment including high-quality mountain bikes, helmets, and gloves. Our guides are certified in first aid and trail maintenance. The adventure includes regular breaks for hydration and snacks, with options for half-day or full-day excursions. For full-day trips, we include a delicious local lunch at a scenic spot along the route.

Safety Information

Safety is our top priority. All participants receive comprehensive safety briefing before the ride. Our guides are certified in first aid and trail safety. We provide high-quality safety equipment including helmets and gloves. Minimum age is 12 years. Participants should have basic cycling ability. We assess trail conditions daily and adjust routes accordingly. All bikes are professionally maintained and checked before each ride.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable athletic clothing
  • Closed-toe shoes suitable for cycling
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Water bottle (we provide refills)
  • Change of clothes (optional)
  • Small backpack for personal items
  • Camera or phone for photos (secured in provided bag)

Frequently Asked Questions

What skill level do I need?
We cater to all skill levels from complete beginners to experienced riders. Our guides will assess your ability and choose trails that match your experience. Beginners will start with gentle trails and receive basic instruction, while advanced riders can tackle more technical terrain.
What type of bikes do you provide?
We use high-quality full-suspension mountain bikes suitable for Sri Lankan terrain. Our fleet includes various frame sizes to ensure proper fit. All bikes are professionally maintained and serviced regularly. We also provide helmets and gloves for all riders.
What happens if it rains?
Light rain usually doesn't affect our rides (and can make some trails more fun!). In case of heavy rain or thunderstorms, we may postpone or cancel for safety reasons. You'll be offered an alternative date or full refund. Some trails may be modified based on weather conditions.
Can I bring my own bike?
Yes, you're welcome to bring your own mountain bike. We offer a 15% discount for riders using their own equipment. Please ensure your bike is in good working condition and suitable for off-road trails. Our guides can perform a basic safety check before the ride.
