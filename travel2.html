Our waterfall abseiling adventure takes you to some of the most beautiful cascades in the Kithulgala area. Safety Information

Your safety is our top priority. All participants receive a comprehensive safety briefing before starting. Our guides are certified in first aid and rescue techniques. We provide high-quality safety equipment including helmets, harnesses, and ropes. Minimum age is 12 years. Participants should have a reasonable level of fitness and be comfortable with heights.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothing that can get wet
  • Secure footwear with good grip (will get wet)
  • Swimwear to wear underneath
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Change of clothes and towel
  • Waterproof camera (optional)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is waterfall abseiling safe for beginners?
Yes, our abseiling trips are designed with beginners in mind. We provide thorough safety briefings, training sessions, and all necessary equipment. Our professional guides are with you at all times, and we only conduct descents when conditions are safe. Many of our participants are first-timers.
What is the minimum age for waterfall abseiling?
The minimum age is 12 years old. Participants under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. We assess each participant's comfort level with heights before proceeding with the activity.
What should I wear for abseiling?
Wear comfortable, quick-drying clothing that allows freedom of movement. Secure footwear with good grip is essential - water shoes or sturdy sandals with straps work well. Avoid loose clothing or jewelry that could get caught in equipment. We provide all technical gear including helmets and harnesses.
Do I need any prior experience?
No prior experience is necessary. Our guides will teach you everything you need to know during the training session. We start with easier descents and progress to more challenging ones as you gain confidence. The activity is suitable for people with average fitness levels.
