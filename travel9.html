NIGHT CAMPING ADVENTURE Available Now

Experience the magic of sleeping under the stars in Kithulgala's pristine jungle environment. Our night camping adventure offers a perfect blend of comfort and wilderness experience. Set up camp by the Kelani River, enjoy a traditional campfire dinner, listen to the sounds of the jungle at night, and wake up to birdsong and a refreshing river swim. Our campsite is carefully selected for safety and beauty, with comfortable tents and all necessary amenities provided.

Camping Highlights

  • Premium waterproof tents with comfortable sleeping mats
  • Traditional campfire with marshmallows and storytelling
  • Delicious Sri Lankan-style barbecue dinner
  • Morning tea/coffee with light breakfast by the river
  • Guided night walk to spot nocturnal wildlife (optional)
  • Safe swimming spots in the Kelani River
  • Basic toilet and washing facilities at campsite

Our night camping experience begins in the late afternoon with camp setup and orientation. You'll have time to explore the surroundings and enjoy a refreshing swim before sunset. As darkness falls, we'll gather around the campfire for dinner, stories, and stargazing. Our guides will share fascinating information about the local ecosystem and wildlife. After a restful night in your tent (with mosquito nets provided), you'll wake to the sounds of the jungle and enjoy a simple breakfast before packing up.

We provide all necessary camping equipment including tents, sleeping mats, mosquito nets, and basic lighting. You'll just need to bring personal items like a sleeping bag (or we can provide one for a small fee), toiletries, and appropriate clothing. Our campsite is located in a safe, scenic area with easy access to the river but protected from wildlife. The experience is suitable for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.

Safety Information

Our campsite is carefully selected for safety and comfort. We maintain a safe distance from the river while still offering easy access. The area is regularly checked for wildlife activity, and we take all necessary precautions. Our guides are trained in first aid and emergency procedures. Tents are equipped with mosquito nets, and we provide safe drinking water. A basic first aid kit is always available at the campsite. In case of unexpected heavy rain, we have contingency plans including alternative sheltered locations.

What to Bring

  • Sleeping bag (or rent from us)
  • Comfortable clothing for warm days and cooler nights
  • Swimwear and towel
  • Toiletries and personal medications
  • Flashlight or headlamp
  • Insect repellent
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Camera (optional)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is camping safe with wildlife around?
Yes, our campsite is carefully selected to minimize wildlife encounters. We maintain a clean campsite (no food in tents), use proper food storage, and position tents in safe areas. While you might hear jungle sounds at night, dangerous wildlife keeps its distance. Our guides are trained to handle any situation and will brief you on safety protocols upon arrival.
What happens if it rains?
We monitor weather closely and will advise you if severe weather is expected. Light rain usually doesn't affect the experience as we have rain shelters and waterproof tents. In case of heavy rain forecast, we may reschedule or offer an alternative sheltered camping location. You'll be informed of any changes in advance.
Are there toilet facilities?
Yes, we provide basic but clean toilet facilities at the campsite. These are simple eco-friendly toilets with privacy tents. For washing, we have fresh water available. While it's not luxury accommodation, we ensure basic hygiene standards are maintained.
Can we do this with young children?
Absolutely! Many families with children as young as 4-5 years enjoy our camping experience. We can provide smaller tents for families and adjust activities to suit children. The campsite is safe for kids, though they should always be supervised near the river. Children under 6 stay free when sharing a tent with parents.
